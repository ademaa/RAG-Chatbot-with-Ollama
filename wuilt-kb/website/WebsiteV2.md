Add Row

## How to customize your section background? (v2)
*Category: مظهر | الموقع *

Add a color as a background


You can change the background of your website's sections by either changing its color or adding a background image


Step 1 
: 
On the right side of each section of your website, you can see the gear  
"Edit background"


Step 2 : 
You can choose whether to change the color by clicking on the white box below 
"background-color" 
and choosing the color you like for that section 




💡
Try to choose a bright, closer-to-white color that is not darker than the text to pop up your section with little changes




Step 3 :
 
Now click apply to preview your new section color and autosave your changes




💡Remember, you can always revert back to the default background settings by clicking on 
"Reset section background






This is what the section looks like after we changed its background color 👇






Add an image as a background


Step 1 : 
 You can also change the background image of the section by clicking on 
"Image"


Step 2 : 
Click on 
"Choose image"
 to choose a picture from previously uploaded assets or upload a new one


Step 3 : 
In this case, you can choose to add an overlay color to blur the picture and make the text pop out without going through every text box and changing its color 




Step 4 : 
Check the 
"Color Overlay" 
box and pick a lighter color. It may take a while until you find the perfect overlay




Step 5 : 
Make sure to change the transparency of the color from the white bar underneath the color selection bar
