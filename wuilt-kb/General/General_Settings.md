## How to purchase a domain |Store
*Category: المتجر الالكترونى *

**
Step 1:
**
 From the dashboard, click on the domain


**
Step 2:
**
 Click on Buy a new domain name


**
Step 3:
**
 Enter the domain name you need to purchase


**
Step 4:
**
 Search for the domain name you want


**
Step 5:
**
 Purchase the domain if it is available 







⚠️ If it is not available, you can choose between the suggested ranges







**
Step 6:
**
 Enter your data


**
Step 7:
**
 Enter your credit card information





Follow the steps from here 👇

## How to buy a new domain | Website
*Category: الموقع الالكترونى *

Step 1: From the dashboard, click on settings then domain 


Step 2: Click on Buy a new domain name


Step 3: Enter the domain name you wish to purchase


Step 4: Search for the domain name you want


Step 5: Purchase the domain if it is available





Step 6: Enter your data


Step 7: Enter your credit card information





Follow the steps here 👇

Step 2 : Then click on Connect Existing Domain 





Step 3 : Type your existing domain, then click on connect
