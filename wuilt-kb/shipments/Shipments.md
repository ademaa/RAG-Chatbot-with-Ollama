## How to calculate your shipment rate
- To determine the chargeable weight, we need to compare the actual weight and the volumetric weight. The higher value is used as the chargeable weight.





- To calculate the volumetric weigh, use the following formula: 










- 
**
Example on how to calculate the exact shipping rate:
**







- 
**
Shipment Actual Weight:
**
 2 kg




- 
**
Package Size:
**
 20 x 30 x 20 cm




- 
**
Base Shipping Rate:
**
 69 EGP (for up to 2 kg)




- 
**
Extra Weight Rate:
**
 3.4 EGP per additional kg








**
Step 1: Calculate the Volumetric Weight
**






Volumetric Weight = 20×30×20 = 12,000 / 3000 = 4 kg





**
Step 2: Determine the Chargeable Weight
**


Since the 
**
volumetric weight (4 kg)
**
 is higher than the 
**
actual weight (2 kg)
**
, the chargeable weight will be 
**
4 kg
**
.







**
Step 3: Calculate the Total Shipping Cost
**




- 
**
Base Shipping Rate (up to 2 kg):
**
 69 EGP




- 
**
Extra Weight (4 kg - 2 kg = 2 kg):
**
 2 kg × 3.4 EGP = 6.8 EGP






**
Total Shipping Cost:
**




Total Cost = 69 EGP + 6.8 EGP = 
**
75.8 EGP
**

💡Note: here is how the payout cycle works:

**
Payout Frequency:
**
 Payouts to merchants are processed weekly, specifically on every Tuesday.

Withdrawal Request Deadline: 
**
To receive a Tuesday payout, submit withdrawal requests by Sunday at 5 PM. Requests after this deadline will be processed the following Tuesday.


**
Flexibility:
**
 Merchants can request withdrawals anytime, but funds are disbursed on Tuesdays based on the Sunday 5 PM cutoff.
